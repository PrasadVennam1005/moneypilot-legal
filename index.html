Privacy Policy for MoneyPilot

Last updated: June 05, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect transaction data, budget information, and profile details that you provide voluntarily within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide financial insights, track your budget, and improve app functionality.

3. Data Syncing

If you sign in with Google, your data is synced exclusively with your own private Google Drive/Sheets. We do not have access to your private spreadsheet.

4. AI Analysis

All AI analysis happens locally on your device; your financial records are never sent to external AI servers.

5. Security

We use SQLCipher encryption to protect your data locally.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact the developer via the app's Help section.
