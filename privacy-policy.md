# Privacy Policy for MoneyPilot

Last Updated: June 2026

## Introduction

MoneyPilot ("the App") is a personal finance management application designed to help users track expenses, income, budgets, investments, loans, and financial insights.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may collect:

* Name
* Email address
* Profile picture

This information is used solely for authentication and personalization.

### Financial Information

The App may store information you voluntarily enter, including:

* Expenses
* Income records
* Budgets
* Investments
* Loan information
* Notes and descriptions
* Categories and payment methods

### Device Information

The App may collect limited technical information required for functionality, such as:

* Device model
* Android version
* App version

No advertising identifiers are collected for advertising purposes.

## How Your Data Is Stored

### Local Storage

Financial records are stored locally on your device using an encrypted database.

### Google Sheets Synchronization

If you enable synchronization, your financial data is stored in a Google Spreadsheet associated with your Google account.

The spreadsheet is created and owned by you.

We do not maintain separate copies of your financial data on our servers.

### Offline Operation

The App is designed to function offline. Data entered while offline is stored locally and synchronized when an internet connection becomes available.

## Google Services

The App uses Google services including:

* Google Sign-In
* Google Sheets API
* Google Drive API (if enabled)

Use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements where applicable.

## AI Features

The App may provide optional AI-powered features such as:

* Expense analysis
* Financial insights
* Voice expense entry
* Spending summaries

If on-device AI is enabled:

* Processing occurs locally on your device.
* Financial data is not transmitted to external AI providers.

If cloud AI features are introduced in future releases, users will be informed and consent will be obtained before any data is transmitted.

## Receipt Scanning

If you use receipt scanning:

* Images are processed to extract transaction information.
* Receipt images are only stored if you choose to save them.
* OCR processing may occur locally on the device.

## Voice Input

If you use voice expense entry:

* Voice data is processed through Android speech recognition services.
* Processing behavior is governed by your device and Google settings.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information is shared only:

* With Google services you authorize
* To provide synchronization functionality
* When required by law

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

* Encrypted local storage
* Secure authentication
* Secure network communication
* Protected access to synchronized data

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Your Rights

You may:

* Access your data
* Modify your data
* Delete your data
* Disable synchronization
* Sign out of the application

Deleting the application removes locally stored data from your device.

Data synchronized to Google Sheets remains under your control and can be deleted directly from your Google account.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
